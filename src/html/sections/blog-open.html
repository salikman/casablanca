From energizing classics to innovative creations inspired by the rhythms we play, there's a drink to match every dancer's taste.

Step into Casablanca and immerse yourself in the most exhilarating dance experience in Prague. Nestled in the heart of the city, each night with us is an open invitation to dance, to celebrate, and to weave unforgettable memories that echo long after the music fades.

So, to those who crave the thrill of dance and the warmth of shared moments, Casablanca beckons. Join us for a night where you're not just a guest, but the star of a celebration that pulses with life. The perfect night awaits—let's make memories that dance on forever.
